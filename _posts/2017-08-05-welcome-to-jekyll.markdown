# Xiao Mian 6 mins, serving 4



## Ingredients:
Angel hair pasta: 1lb
Yopolazi: 1tbsp
Soy Sauce: 2 tbsp
Vinegar: 1tbsp
Salt: 1tsp

## Process:
Boil water in a pot
Put pasta in and keep boiling for 5 mins
Drain pasta and add your choice of steamed vegetables
Add Yopolazi, soy sauce, vinegar and salt, stir mix
